=== Zbc: Carry-less multiplication

[NOTE,caption=Frozen]
====
The Zbc extension is frozen.
====

*clmul* produces the lower half of the carry-less product and *clmulh* produces the upper half of the 2&#x2715;XLEN carry-less product.
